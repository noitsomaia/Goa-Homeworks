Allen Iverson

Allen Iverson is an American former professional basketball player. Nicknamed "The Answer" he played 14 seasons in the National Basketball Association (NBA) at both the shooting guard and point guard positions. Iverson Won NBA Rookie of the Year Award in 1997 and was an 11-time NBA All-Star, won the All-Star game MVP award in 2001 and 2005, and was the NBA's Valuable Player (MVP ) in 2001. Iverson is often regarded as one of the greatest scorers and one of the most influential players in NBA history.
